Valerie Thomas

The Woman Who Invented The Illusion Transmitter (or 3D image)

Here a time line tribute to Valerie Thomas

  • 1943 -Born in Maryland, United States.
  • 1964 - Graduated with Honors with a degree in physics.
  • 1964 - Began working for NASA as a data analys.
  • 1970 - 1981 - She oversaw the creation of the Landsat program.
  • 1974 - Thomas headed a team with an unprecedented scientific project that demonstrated the feasibility of using space technology to automate the process of forecasting wheat yields around the world.
  • 1985 - 1990 - Manage large computer setups, projects where more computers connected.
  • 1995 - She retired from NASA

Valerie Thomas is credited with being the TRUE creator of the 3D imagery
